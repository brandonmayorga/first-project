Before the NBA

D'Angelo Russell was born February 23, 1996. His hometown is Louisville, Kentucky. He had a successful high school career in which he was named McDonald's All-American player. He then committed to Ohio State for his college choice. He played one year of college basketball and declared for the NBA draft instead of foregoing three more years of college.

Draft

D'Angelo Russell was drafted to the NBA in 2015 as the second overall pick in the first round. He was drafted to the Los Angeles Lakers. He was drafted from Ohio State University. He was a freshman in college when he was declared for the NBA draft. D'Angelo plays as a point guard and shooting guard for his current team.
