D'Angelo's Thoughts On Coming Season

D'Angelo says he wants to get stronger to compete with other point guards in the league. He also thinks that the team were all trying to prove something individually. But he thinks that they can be more successful if they start to play as a team. D'Angelo is inspired by Kobe Bryant's work ethic and he wants to be able to work as hard as him. D'Angelo says he still feels like he has to prove that he can compete with the rest of the players in the NBA and not just be average.
