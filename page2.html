First Season

D'Angelo Russell's first season with the LA Lakers didn't go to well. The team all together ended the season with 17 wins and 65 losses. He played in 80 of the 82 total games in the seeason. He had an average of 13.2 PPG for the whole season. He got the chance to gain more experience from players like Kobe.

Summer League 2016

In the summer league of 2016 the Lakers and D'Angelo did alright. They went 3-2 and was 8th in standings out of all 24 teams. D'Angelo became like the in-game leader during the summer league. The first game Russell played in the summer league he scored 20 points, got 11 rebounds, got 6 assists, and had 3 steals. He even got had the game winning 3-pointer against the 76ers to win 70-69.
